FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the steps for signing up to RVL system?
    RVL sign up procedure consists of two steps: Initially, the user will have to complete the 4 steps of registration and submit it successfully. Next, user will receive an email for account setup. After completing the 5 steps for account setup, and device authorization, user will be able to login to RVL portal using RVL Credentials
  • What does “liveliness detection failed” mean?
    This error means the system did not detect any movement while capturing the image. Please follow the instructions while taking the picture for e-KYC.
  • I haven’t setup my password but clicked activate account. How do I setup my password now?
    "To reset credentials follow the following steps: 1. Go to the RVL authenticator app. 2. On the menu bar, click ""Reset Credentials"". 3. Wait to receive the account setup email. 4. Once the account setup email is received, proceed with setting up your RVL account"
  • How do I create a new profile for my account?
    To create a new user profile, user must login to the account created. Go to "my account>user profiles>create user profile"
  • Can I sign bulk documents in one go?
    RVL system supports signing maximum 70 documents in one go
  • After validation, the reports say, “the signature is valid, but the signature has some problems”. What does this mean?
    This error message appears when the signers source are not recognized by RVL
  • How can third party validate any signed document?
    Any third party can validate documents using the "Validate" feature of RVL. Or, they can also validate using adobe PDF reader by opening the signature panel>show signer's certificate and check the certificate validity and certificate path.
  • I forgot my password. How do I reset my password?
    "In such scenario, you have to try to reset your credentials. To reset credentials follow the following steps: 1. Go to the RVL authenticator app. 2. On the menu bar, click ""Reset Credentials"". 3. Wait to receive the account setup email. 4. Once the account setup email is received, proceed with setting up your RVL account "
  • I lost my phone. How do I setup my account to my new device?
    Enter the RVL portal using light mode. Go to "My account>account>reset credentials" and continue with account setup.
